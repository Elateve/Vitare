The Location

Jardinets de Gracia -- Barcelona

~250 sqm in one of Barcelona's most prestigious and strategically connected neighbourhoods. Three reasons this is the right address.

01

Hotel Proximity

Walking distance from Barcelona's top 5-star hotels -- Mandarin Oriental, Almanac, Majestic, Cotton House, El Palace. These are our referral partners. Being close enough for a concierge to send a guest over in 10 minutes is the difference between a conversion and a "maybe next time."

02

Expats & High-Profile Locals

The Gracia corridor is home to Barcelona's highest concentration of affluent expat professionals, tech executives from the 22@ district, and established local families. These are people who already spend on wellness, performance, and longevity -- and they live within a 10-minute walk.

03

Iconic & Timeless

Jardinets de Gracia never goes out of fashion. It sits at the intersection of Eixample elegance and Gracia character -- prestigious without being flashy. The kind of address that gives a brand credibility from day one and never needs to be explained or justified.
