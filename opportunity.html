The Consumer Has Changed

Millennials and Gen Z are not waiting for symptoms to appear. They are proactively seeking blood biomarker panels, epigenetic testing, and personalised nutrition protocols years before they are told to. The concept of "healthspan" -- not just lifespan -- is driving a consumer shift toward diagnostic wellness that is directly in VITARE' lane.

73% of affluent consumers aged 30-60 say they would spend EUR 100-500 on a comprehensive health diagnostic if it came with actionable, personalised protocols (Julius Baer Global Wealth & Lifestyle Report, 2025).

Barcelona Is the Ideal Market

Barcelona's unique demographic cocktail -- 100,000+ expatriates, a thriving 22@ tech district, a world-class triathlon and padel community, and 20M+ annual tourists including a significant wellness-motivated segment -- creates a perfect early-adopter environment.

There is currently no all-in-one longevity club with diagnostic testing, contrast therapy, red light therapy, IV infusion, and a membership social model within the city. The gap is visible, the timing is optimal.

Location Strategy

Where in Barcelona -- The Decision

Location is everything. The ideal VITARE space must deliver three things simultaneously: affluent foot traffic, the right postcode for brand perception, and operational proximity to hotel partners for the retreat flywheel.

Primary Target
Jardins de Gracia /
Carrer de Gracia Corridor

The hidden gem of Barcelona's premium wellness real estate. Between the top of Paseo de Gracia and the beginning of Gracia, this corridor offers the brand elegance of Eixample without the tourist density -- and immediate proximity to the most aspirational residential blocks in the city.

  • Home to BCN's highest-density affluent residential -- expat professionals, executives
  • Walking distance from Diagonal and top of Paseo de Gracia hotel cluster
  • Ground floor + basement availability in heritage Eixample buildings (approx 300-500m2)
  • Proximity to Turo Park -- Barcelona's premier outdoor social space for the target demo
  • Quiet but prestigious -- ideal for the private-club feel VITARE must cultivate
Alternative A
Top of Paseo de Gracia
(Diagonal / Provenca)

Maximum brand visibility and tourist capture. Highest rents in the city. The trade-off: noise, density, and the risk of appearing too commercial for the intimate club experience VITARE is selling.

  • Unmatched brand halo -- best address in Barcelona
  • Direct access to 5-star hotel guest flow
  • Strong expat and digital nomad density
Alternative B
Sant Gervasi /
Turo Park Edge

Barcelona's most underrated premium residential neighbourhood. Upper Eixample meets Gracia meets old-money Barcelona. A slightly smaller but fiercely loyal member base.

  • Most affluent residential neighbourhood in BCN
  • Parking availability -- critical for older affluent members
  • Significantly lower rent per m2 vs Paseo de Gracia
Hotel Partnership Priority
Hotel-Embedded
Launch Model

If launching within a hotel, target: Mandarin Oriental BCN (Paseo de Gracia), Soho House BCN, Almanac Barcelona, or Cotton House Hotel. All are within the Jardins de Gracia corridor.

  • Zero zoning/permitting wait -- immediate launch
  • Built-in tourist audience from day one
  • Revenue share replaces fixed rent risk
