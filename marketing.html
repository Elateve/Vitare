Marketing Strategy

The "Get to the Root" Campaign

ROOTS' marketing philosophy is simple: give people a result they could not get anywhere else, for free, and let the result do the selling. The blood test is not a loss-leader. It is the most powerful marketing asset in the business.

01

Influencer Diagnostic Programme

Partner with 20-30 Barcelona-based micro-influencers (10k-150k followers) in fitness, nutrition, lifestyle, and expat communities. Offer them a complimentary full diagnostic. Their authentic reaction to their own results is the content. #MyROOTSResults becomes a discovery campaign. Budget: EUR 15k/year in product value.

02

Precision Digital Targeting

Target: Women 30-55 within 5km of Eixample/Gracia. Interests: Oura Ring, WHOOP, functional medicine, longevity, epigenetics, intermittent fasting. Men: high-income executives in 22@, padel, triathlon. Lead magnet: "Get Your Free ROOTS Health Scan" -- a 3-minute skin + body scan with a branded results PDF.

03

Community Referral Engine

Every ROOTS member receives 3 "First Blood Panel Free" cards per quarter to give to friends. When that friend joins, the referring member gets a free IV drip or month added to their membership. The refer-a-friend programme in this demographic is extraordinarily powerful -- health is the new dinner party conversation.

04

Athlete + Club Partnerships

Barcelona's padel and triathlon communities are a highly concentrated demographic that maps perfectly to ROOTS' ideal member. Partner with Smash Padel BCN, Triathlon Club Barcelona, and Real Club de Tenis Barcelona as official recovery and diagnostic partner. Branded pop-up labs at post-race events.

05

Founding Member Pre-Launch

Pre-launch "Founding 100" campaign at a 30% discount. Founding members get their name on the lounge wall and a permanent lock-in rate. Creates urgency, validates demand, generates MRR before a single piece of equipment arrives, and creates social proof for the press launch.

06

PR + Editorial Strategy

ROOTS' story is editorial gold: "The Longevity Club That Starts With Your Blood." Pitch to: El Pais Semanal, Vogue Espana, Monocle, Financial Times Life & Arts, Conde Nast Traveller. One cover story in Monocle is worth EUR 300k in ad spend. Target the medical credibility angle -- a registered doctor, real diagnostics, a real longevity concept.
