Marketing Strategy

The "Root of Wellness" Campaign

VITARE's marketing philosophy is simple: give people a result they couldn't get anywhere else, for free, and let the result do the selling. The blood test is not a loss-leader. It is the most powerful marketing asset in the business.

📱

Influencer Diagnostic Programme

Partner with 20–30 Barcelona-based micro-influencers (10k–150k followers) in fitness, nutrition, lifestyle, and expat communities. Offer them a complimentary full diagnostic. Their authentic reaction to their own results is the content. #MyVITAREResults becomes a discovery campaign. Budget: €15k/year in product value.

🎯

Precision Digital Targeting

Target: Women 30–55 within 5km of Eixample/Gràcia. Interests: Oura Ring, WHOOP, functional medicine, longevity, epigenetics, intermittent fasting. Men: high-income executives in 22@, padel, triathlon. Lead magnet: "Get Your Free VITARE Health Scan" — a 3-minute skin + body scan with a branded results PDF.

🤝

Community Referral Engine

Every VITARE member receives 3 "First Blood Panel Free" cards per quarter to give to friends. When that friend joins, the referring member gets a free IV drip or month added to their membership. The refer-a-friend programme in this demographic is extraordinarily powerful — health is the new dinner party conversation.

🏃

Athlete + Club Partnerships

Barcelona's padel and triathlon communities are a highly concentrated demographic that maps perfectly to VITARE's ideal member. Partner with Smash Padel BCN, Triathlon Club Barcelona, and Real Club de Tenis Barcelona as official recovery and diagnostic partner. Branded pop-up labs at post-race events.

🏆

Founding Member Pre-Launch

Pre-launch "Founding 100" campaign at a 30% discount. Founding members get their name on the lounge wall and a permanent lock-in rate. Creates urgency, validates demand, generates MRR before a single piece of equipment arrives, and creates social proof for the press launch.

📰

PR + Editorial Strategy

VITARE's story is editorial gold: "The Longevity Club That Starts With Your Blood." Pitch to: El País Semanal, Vogue España, Monocle, Financial Times Life & Arts, Condé Nast Traveller. One cover story in Monocle is worth €300k in ad spend. Target the medical credibility angle — a registered doctor, real diagnostics, a real longevity concept.
