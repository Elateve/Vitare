What We're Building

Numbers -- Transparency Notes

The Detail Behind the Ask

Three points worth flagging for the investor conversation. The numbers are intentionally conservative and the reasoning behind each line is documented.

01

Why EUR 600k

A gold-plated "best standard" build came in at EUR 641k -- driven by a medical-grade AHA 2.0 ATA HBOT chamber (EUR 70k vs a mild 1.3 ATA pod at EUR 55k) and all three blood analysers including the Piccolo Xpress. Swapping to an OxyHelp HBOT (EUR 50k) and dropping Piccolo saves ~EUR 25k. EUR 600k is the balanced mid-point with a built-in EUR 13k contingency for Barcelona construction overruns.

02

Staff Coverage During Ramp

The 6-month OPEX buffer covers the full Phase 2 team (doctor, nurse, receptionist, assistant + Elateve base fee, ~EUR 18k/month all-in) through the ramp period. Payroll becomes self-funding at roughly 40 members -- a threshold comparable Barcelona clubs hit within 2-3 months of opening. By month 6 at 120 members, payroll is only ~31% of membership revenue.

03

Legal -- What's Inside the EUR 60k

The EUR 60k legal line actually bundles three items: lawyers + company formation (EUR 24k), AEMPS medical registration + sanitary permits (EUR 18k), and the Barcelona activity licence (EUR 18k). Split transparently so the investor sees exactly what each euro buys. Total matches the original figure, with full traceability.
