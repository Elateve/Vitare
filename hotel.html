Hotel Referral Partnerships

The Referral Network

Biohacking Studio operates as a fully owned standalone centre in Barcelona. Premium hotels become our most powerful customer acquisition channel: their concierge desks and wellness teams refer guests directly into the centre, and we share the upside on every booking. The exact referral percentage will be agreed individually with each hotel partner in the next phase.

Zero

No Capital from the Hotel

Hotels offer their guests a curated wellness option without spending a euro. They simply add Biohacking Studio to their concierge menu and in-room compendium. We handle everything else -- branding, bookings, transport, and the full guest experience inside our centre.

Per-Visit

Referral Commission

Each referred guest generates a commission for the hotel. The exact percentage and tier structure will be negotiated case-by-case with each property in the next phase, based on volume, exclusivity, and the hotel's positioning. Higher volume = higher share.

Premium

Guest Experience Upgrade

For 5-star hotels, having a longevity centre to refer guests to is a meaningful upgrade to their wellness offering -- without operational complexity. It positions the hotel in the editorial conversation around longevity travel.

Tracked

Transparent Reporting

Every referral is tagged at booking. Hotels receive a monthly statement of referred guests, services purchased, and commission earned. Clean, automated, and auditable -- no surprises.

Target Partners

The Hotels We Want to Work With

We are focused on premium properties whose guests already value wellness, longevity, and high-touch experiences. The shortlist below represents the type of hotel partner that fits the Biohacking Studio brand -- both in Barcelona and in the wider Catalonia / Balearics region for visiting guests.

01

5-Star Barcelona Centre

Mandarin Oriental, Soho House, Almanac, Cotton House, Majestic, El Palace -- the properties whose guests already pay for wellness, spa, and concierge experiences. These hotels are inside or within walking distance of Jardinets de Gracia.

02

Wellness-Forward Resorts

Properties with an existing wellness identity that lack a medical-longevity offering. Their guests are pre-qualified for Biohacking Studio: they already value diagnostics, recovery, and contrast therapy.

03

Boutique & Lifestyle Hotels

Smaller premium hotels that compete on experience rather than scale. A Biohacking Studio referral is a differentiator their concierge team can offer that competitors cannot.

How It Works

From Hotel Lobby to Biohacking Studio Centre

1
Discovery
A guest at a partner hotel asks the concierge about wellness options
Biohacking Studio sits at the top of the hotel's wellness recommendations alongside spa and fitness. The concierge introduces it as the city's premium longevity centre -- diagnostics, IV drips, contrast therapy, recovery.
2
Booking
The concierge books the guest directly through the Biohacking Studio referral portal
A simple booking link tagged to the hotel. The system records the referral source automatically -- no manual tracking, no disputed commissions.
3
Experience
The guest visits the centre and goes through the Biohacking Studio protocol
Diagnostics, IV drip, contrast therapy, red light, recovery lounge -- whatever they have booked. They leave with a personalised report and a clear sense of what to do next.
4
Settlement
The hotel earns a commission on every referred booking
Monthly statement, monthly payout. The commission percentage is set in the referral agreement signed in the next phase -- tiered by volume so high-performing partners earn more.
Next phase: Once the centre is open and the referral portal is live, Biohacking Studio will approach each target hotel with a tailored proposal. The exact commission percentage, exclusivity terms, and reporting cadence will be agreed individually -- we expect to onboard the first cohort of partner hotels within the first 90 days of operation.
