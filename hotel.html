Part Two — The Retreat Strategy

VITARE On the Road — The Hotel Flywheel

The most powerful customer acquisition channel VITARE has is not Instagram. It is not SEO. It is the moment a hotel guest in Sitges, Menorca, or the Costa Brava puts a VITARE blood test needle in their arm and sees their cortisol results for the first time.

The hotel retreat strategy transforms VITARE's diagnostic offering into a mobile, scalable revenue stream that simultaneously acts as a member acquisition engine for the Barcelona centre. Here is how the flywheel works.

1
Partnership
VITARE partners with 5-star wellness hotels within a 2-hour radius of Barcelona
Target hotels: Torre del Remei (Girona), Can Bonastre (Penedès), Sha Wellness Clinic (Alicante), Son Brull (Mallorca), and 10–15 high-end wellness-forward properties across Catalonia and the Balearics. We sell them packaged "VITARE Wellness Days."
2
On-Property Experience
The "VITARE Reveal" day at the hotel — €350–500 per guest
A VITARE nurse visits the hotel property for 1–2 days per month. Hotel guests can book a VITARE Wellness Reveal package including blood panel, skin scan, body composition analysis, 1× IV drip or vitamin shot, and a personalised protocol document. Packaged, beautiful, transformational.
3
The Tie-Back
The protocol document points to Barcelona for the follow-up treatment
"Your cortisol is elevated and your Vitamin D is critically deficient. Your personalised VITARE protocol recommends: 8 weeks of contrast therapy (sauna + cold plunge), 4× NAD+ IV infusions, and reformer Pilates 3x/week. This is available at VITARE Barcelona — a 2-hour drive or 35-minute flight from your home." The guest already trusts VITARE. The conversion writes itself.
4
Airport Capture
The VITARE Airport Drop-In — for guests connecting through El Prat
For international guests heading home via Barcelona El Prat, VITARE offers a "Lay-over Longevity" package. Book 3 hours at VITARE Barcelona on departure day: contrast therapy + blood panel + IV boost + bar lunch. Position it as the world's best airport layover. Partner with hotel concierge desks to pre-book. €180 all-in.
5
Membership Conversion
Remote members + international digital memberships
For guests who live outside Barcelona, VITARE offers a Digital Longevity Membership (€95/month): access to the VITARE app, quarterly at-home blood testing kit, supplement protocol updates, and 4 free day passes annually when visiting Barcelona. These members remain in the VITARE ecosystem, refer Barcelona-resident friends, and upgrade to full membership when they relocate.
