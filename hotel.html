Model A -- Hotel Partnership

The Hotel Pitch

When we sit down with hotel GM and ownership, we are not asking them for a favour. We are bringing them a product that increases their RevPAR, fills their dead space, and gives them a turnkey medical-wellness offering they could never build themselves.

+18%

ADR Uplift

Hotels with premium, destination-worthy wellness facilities command an average 12-22% higher Average Daily Rate over comparable properties without. ROOTS' medical-longevity positioning is the most premium category in the wellness space.

Year-Round

Off-Season Revenue

Barcelona's tourism dips November-February. A membership-driven longevity club with 150+ local members ensures the hotel lobby, cafe, and wellness spaces remain buzzing 52 weeks a year -- not just 32.

Zero

Operational Effort

The hotel provides the real estate and utilities. ROOTS (managed by Elateve) provides everything else -- staffing, medical licensing, equipment, branding, and operations. The hotel earns 20-30% revenue share with zero management burden.

Turnkey

Brand Differentiation

A medical longevity club is a tier above any standard spa or gym offering. It positions the hotel in the editorial conversation around wellness travel -- press coverage, influencer visits, and experiential travel bookings follow.

Part Two -- The Retreat Strategy

ROOTS On the Road -- The Hotel Flywheel

The most powerful customer acquisition channel ROOTS has is not Instagram. It is not SEO. It is the moment a hotel guest puts a ROOTS blood test needle in their arm and sees their cortisol results for the first time.

The hotel retreat strategy transforms ROOTS' diagnostic offering into a mobile, scalable revenue stream that simultaneously acts as a member acquisition engine for the Barcelona centre. Here is how the flywheel works.

1
Partnership
ROOTS partners with 5-star wellness hotels within a 2-hour radius of Barcelona
Target hotels: Torre del Remei (Girona), Can Bonastre (Penedes), Sha Wellness Clinic (Alicante), Son Brull (Mallorca), and 10-15 high-end wellness-forward properties across Catalonia and the Balearics. We sell them packaged "ROOTS Wellness Days."
2
On-Property Experience
The "ROOTS Reveal" day at the hotel -- EUR 350-500 per guest
A ROOTS nurse visits the hotel property for 1-2 days per month. Hotel guests can book a ROOTS Wellness Reveal package including blood panel, skin scan, body composition analysis, 1x IV drip or vitamin shot, and a personalised protocol document. Packaged, beautiful, transformational.
3
The Tie-Back
The protocol document points to Barcelona for the follow-up treatment
"Your cortisol is elevated and your Vitamin D is critically deficient. Your personalised ROOTS protocol recommends: 8 weeks of contrast therapy (sauna + cold plunge), 4x NAD+ IV infusions, and reformer Pilates 3x/week. This is available at ROOTS Barcelona." The guest already trusts ROOTS. The conversion writes itself.
4
Airport Capture
The ROOTS Airport Drop-In -- for guests connecting through El Prat
For international guests heading home via Barcelona El Prat, ROOTS offers a "Lay-over Longevity" package. Book 3 hours at ROOTS Barcelona on departure day: contrast therapy + blood panel + IV boost + bar lunch. Position it as the world's best airport layover. Partner with hotel concierge desks to pre-book. EUR 180 all-in.
5
Membership Conversion
Remote members + international digital memberships
For guests who live outside Barcelona, ROOTS offers a Digital Longevity Membership (EUR 95/month): access to the ROOTS app, quarterly at-home blood testing kit, supplement protocol updates, and 4 free day passes annually when visiting Barcelona. These members remain in the ROOTS ecosystem, refer Barcelona-resident friends, and upgrade to full membership when they relocate.

Retreat Programme Packages

ROOTS sells three packaged offerings to partner hotels -- priced for their specific guest demographics and stay lengths.

REVEAL

Standard blood panel + skin analysis + body composition + 1x IV vitamin drip + personalised protocol document. Perfect for wellness retreat add-on. Hotel net: EUR 87.50 (25% rev share).

Advanced longevity panel including epigenetics + hormones + VO2 max test + 2x IV drips + 60-min consultation with ROOTS medical director + 12-week protocol. The transformational weekend wellness experience.
