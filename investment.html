Section 1 -- Ownership & Entity Structure

How It Works

The investor is buying a managed asset, not a job. Elateve runs everything. The investor owns the entity, holds the contract with the property, and receives 85% of all profit.

Project Entity (NewCo)

100% owned by the Investor. This is the local operating company that signs the agreement with the Hotel or Property Owner. The investor holds full equity.

Management & IP

100% owned by Elateve. Elateve owns the ROOTS brand, the "4 Pillars" methodology, the software/app, and operating manuals. Elateve grants a limited license to the Project Entity.

The Agreement

A Management Services Agreement (MSA) between the Investor's Entity and Elateve. This governs operations, fees, IP usage, and performance benchmarks.

Roles & Responsibilities

PartyResponsibility
Investor 100% of CAPEX + 6-12 months working capital. Holds primary contract with physical site (hotel or landlord). Owns the local entity. Receives 85% of EBITDA.
Elateve Concept & Strategy, Project Management (launch + procurement), Operations (hiring, training, managing day-to-day), Sales & Marketing, IP & Brand licensing. Receives base fee + marketing contribution + 15% success fee on EBITDA.

Section 3 -- IP & Scalability

Intellectual Property & Growth Rights

Elateve retains the brand and methodology. The investor gets a protected, managed location with right of first refusal on expansion.

Brand & Methodology

Elateve retains 100% of ROOTS brand trademarks, the "4 Pillars" methodology, proprietary software/app, and the complete operating manual. These are licensed to the investor's entity under the MSA.

Franchise Rights

Elateve reserves the exclusive right to franchise or license the ROOTS concept globally. The investor's rights are limited to the funded location(s) under their entity.

Right of First Refusal

The investor has RIGHT OF FIRST REFUSAL on any new ROOTS centres -- both within Barcelona and in other cities. This protects the investor's territory and gives them first opportunity to scale.

Management Agreement Terms

Term: 5-10 years. Exclusivity: Investor gets Right of First Refusal for any ROOTS centres within Barcelona or other cities. Termination: Standard "for cause" clauses if Elateve fails performance benchmarks.
