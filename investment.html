Section 1 -- Ownership & Entity Structure

How It Works

The investor funds and owns the centre. The Elateve runs day-to-day operations. Net profit is split 80/20 -- 80% to the investor, 20% to the Elateve -- fully aligned around performance.

Project Entity (NewCo)

The local operating company that signs the lease, holds the licences, and runs the centre. The investor funds the build and provides the working capital.

Investor

Provides 100% of the CAPEX and operating buffer. Receives 80% of net profit. No day-to-day involvement required -- the centre runs without you.

Elateve

Handles operations, hiring, medical compliance, marketing, and reporting. Receives 20% of net profit -- compensated only when the centre is profitable, fully aligned with the investor.

Supplement Partnership Strategy

Biohacking Studio will partner with a premium nutraceutical supplier to offer personalised supplement stacks prescribed from blood panel results. Members receive a curated protocol (Vitamin D3+K2, Magnesium Glycinate, Omega-3, NMN, Creatine, Ashwagandha KSM-66, etc.) that they can purchase directly through Biohacking Studio. Target margin: 35-50% on supplement sales. This is a recurring passive revenue stream that deepens the diagnostic-to-treatment loop.

Partnership Model

We are actively seeking a white-label or co-branded supplement partner. The ideal partner provides pharmaceutical-grade formulations, handles fulfilment and logistics, and allows Biohacking Studio-branded packaging. The supplement protocol is updated quarterly based on re-testing results, creating a built-in recurring purchase cycle. This partnership is a key operational milestone for pre-launch.
