Results-Driven Recommendations

Your Results Shape Your Protocol

Every blood panel result triggers a personalised protocol. Biohacking Studio doesn't guess -- we prescribe specific treatments, supplements, and lifestyle changes based on what your biology actually needs.

When a member's results come back, the Biohacking Studio medical team maps each deficiency and imbalance to a specific combination of in-centre treatments and a personalised supplement stack. This is not generic wellness advice -- it is a data-driven protocol that evolves with every quarterly re-test.

Supplement Recommendations

Based on blood panel results, each member receives a personalised supplement protocol. Biohacking Studio partners with premium-grade nutraceutical suppliers to offer curated stacks: Vitamin D3 + K2, Magnesium Glycinate, Omega-3 (EPA/DHA), Zinc Picolinate, B-Complex, Ashwagandha KSM-66, NMN, Creatine Monohydrate, and more. Members can purchase directly through Biohacking Studio at a margin of 35-50%, or source independently -- the protocol is theirs either way.

Treatment Prescriptions

Each protocol recommends a specific treatment cadence from the Biohacking Studio service menu. For example: elevated cortisol triggers a contrast therapy + red light prescription. Low NAD+ levels trigger an IV NAD+ infusion course. Poor circulation flags hyperbaric oxygen sessions. The blood panel is the map -- the treatments are the directions. This drives repeat visits, deepens trust, and creates a medical-grade retention loop no traditional gym or spa can replicate.

The Service Menu

Treatments & Therapies

The most in-demand longevity and biohacking treatments available today -- all under one roof, all prescribed based on your blood panel results.

01
HBOT

Hyperbaric Oxygen Therapy

Pressurised oxygen chambers (1.3-2.0 ATA) that flood cells with pure O2. Clinically proven to accelerate tissue repair, reduce inflammation, enhance cognitive function, and stimulate stem cell production. One of the fastest-growing treatments in longevity medicine, used by elite athletes and endorsed by leading longevity researchers.

02
RLT

Red Light Therapy

Full-body photobiomodulation panels (630-850nm wavelengths). Stimulates mitochondrial ATP production, accelerates collagen synthesis, reduces joint pain, and improves skin elasticity. 20-minute sessions. One of the highest-margin, lowest-cost treatments in the biohacking space -- and one of the most requested.

03
COLD

Cold Plunge + Contrast Therapy

Commercial-grade cold plunges at 4-8 degrees C paired with Finnish saunas at 85 degrees C. The contrast protocol is clinically proven to reduce systemic inflammation, elevate HRV, boost norepinephrine by 200-300%, and dramatically improve sleep quality and mood. The signature Biohacking Studio walk-in experience.

04
IV

IV Drip Lounge

NAD+ infusions (250-500mg), Myers Cocktail, Glutathione, High-Dose Vitamin C, Performance Drips, and custom protocols. IV therapy delivers 100% bioavailability -- bypassing the gut for immediate cellular absorption. The highest-ticket, highest-retention service in the centre. Prescribed based on blood panel deficiencies.

05
PRESS

Compression Therapy

Full-leg pneumatic compression (NormaTec or equivalent). Enhances lymphatic drainage, reduces DOMS, accelerates recovery between training sessions. 30-minute sessions. Widely adopted by professional athletes and increasingly demanded by the biohacking consumer. Very low operating cost, high perceived value.

06
O3

Ozone Therapy

Ozone (O3) administration for immune modulation, enhanced oxygen utilisation, and detoxification support. Growing rapidly in the European longevity clinic market. Can be administered via IV (MAH -- Major Autohemotherapy), insufflation, or topical application. Pairs powerfully with Biohacking Studio's diagnostic model -- prescribed when inflammation or immune markers are flagged.

07
EMS

EMS Training

Electrical muscle stimulation suits delivering a full-body strength workout in 20 minutes. Activates 90% of muscle fibres simultaneously vs 60% in conventional training. Ideal for time-poor professionals. Growing fast in the Barcelona fitness market. Low space requirement, high perceived value, strong upsell from membership.

08
CRYO

Localised Cryotherapy

Targeted cryotherapy for specific injury sites, joints, and inflammation zones. Complements the full-body cold plunge with precision treatment for chronic pain, sports injuries, and post-surgical recovery. Quick 10-minute sessions that can be added to any visit.
