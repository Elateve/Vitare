The Brand

A Name Everyone Understands

The name had to be universal — one word, memorised instantly, and weighted with meaning. Here are three candidates and our clear recommendation.

Latin: "To Live Fully"

VITARE

From the Latin vita (life) and the suffix -are (to embody). Sounds like "vitality" without stating it. Feels medical, luxurious, and unmistakably intentional. Easy to say in Spanish, English, Catalan, French, and German. Domain-ready. Timeless.

Latin: "Source" + "Origin"

ORÍGINE

Captures the "getting to the root of your wellness" narrative beautifully. Strong in French and Italian luxury context. Slightly harder to own globally and may read as a beauty brand rather than a medical longevity club.

Greek: "Complete" + Health

HOLOS

From the Greek holos (whole, entire). Clean and modern. Works globally. Risks confusion with existing spa brands in the EU market and lacks the premium medical gravitas VITARE commands.
