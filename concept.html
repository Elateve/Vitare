The Brand

A Name That Says Everything

The name had to be universal -- one word, memorised instantly, and weighted with meaning. Here are three candidates and our clear recommendation.

English: "The Root Cause"

ROOTS

From the concept of getting to the root cause of health issues through diagnostic blood testing. Universally understood. Feels grounded, natural, and scientific at once. Works in Spanish, English, Catalan, French, and German. Domain-ready. Timeless.

Latin: "Source" + "Origin"

ORIGINE

Captures the "getting to the root of your wellness" narrative beautifully. Strong in French and Italian luxury context. Slightly harder to own globally and may read as a beauty brand rather than a medical longevity club.

Greek: "Complete" + Health

HOLOS

From the Greek holos (whole, entire). Clean and modern. Works globally. Risks confusion with existing spa brands in the EU market and lacks the premium medical gravitas ROOTS commands.

What We Test -- The Full Diagnostic Menu

The "free blood test" draws people in. The results keep them. Here is every test in ROOTS' diagnostic arsenal, from complimentary entry-level scans to full longevity panels:

Complimentary (Always Free)
ROOTS Skin Analysis Scan
AI-powered skin age, hydration, UV damage mapping, collagen density, pore health, and pigmentation score. Takes 3 minutes.
Body Composition Analysis
InBody or Tanita bioimpedance scan: muscle mass, visceral fat index, hydration levels, metabolic age, and biological age indicator.
Blood Pressure + HRV Check
Resting heart rate, blood pressure, heart rate variability -- the simplest window into cardiovascular and nervous system health.
Standard Blood Panel -- EUR 149 (Free with Membership)
Full Hormonal Profile
Testosterone (free + total), estrogen, progesterone, DHEA-S, SHBG, LH, FSH, prolactin. The foundation of energy, mood, and body composition.
Cortisol + Stress Axis
Morning cortisol, cortisol awakening response -- the single most important marker for chronic stress, adrenal function, and sleep quality.
Thyroid Complete (TSH, T3, T4)
The thyroid runs metabolism, weight, energy, skin, and hair. Most GPs test only TSH. ROOTS tests all three fractions plus antibodies.
Inflammation Markers
hs-CRP (high-sensitivity C-reactive protein), homocysteine, IL-6. Chronic silent inflammation is the root of nearly every longevity-related disease.
Metabolic + Insulin Panel
Fasting insulin, HbA1c, glucose, HOMA-IR index. Catches insulin resistance years before a T2D diagnosis arrives.
Nutrient Deficiencies
Vitamin D3, B12, ferritin, magnesium, zinc, folate, omega-3 index. The most commonly missed root causes of fatigue, brain fog, and poor sleep.
Advanced Longevity Panel -- EUR 349 (Available as Add-On)
Epigenetic Biological Age
DNA methylation clock (GrimAge or DunedinPACE). Reveals whether your biological age is younger or older than your calendar age.
Telomere Length Analysis
Measures the length of protective chromosome caps -- one of the most validated markers of cellular aging and longevity potential.
VO2 Max Metabolic Test
The single strongest predictor of all-cause mortality. Establishes the fitness baseline and prescribes the exact training zone for longevity.
Full Lipid + ApoB Panel
LDL particle number, ApoB, Lp(a) -- the cardiovascular markers that predict heart disease 15 years before it manifests.
Gut Microbiome Analysis
At-home stool test revealing microbiome diversity, dysbiosis markers, and personalised probiotic and dietary recommendations.
Heavy Metals + Toxin Load
Mercury, lead, arsenic, cadmium. Toxic burden is a major and massively underdiagnosed contributor to fatigue, cognitive decline, and hormonal disruption.
