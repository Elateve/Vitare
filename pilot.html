The Opportunity

A Ready-Made First Move

We have a unique opportunity to pilot VITARE's core services in Girona through an existing relationship with Tomas -- Tragaluz group -- who is opening a new hotel and has offered to host us on the premises. This is an almost zero-overhead way to start moving, start selling, and start proving the concept before the main investment is committed.

The Location

Tomas's new hotel in Girona, operated under the Tragaluz umbrella -- one of Spain's most respected hospitality groups. The hotel provides the space. VITARE provides the services, the branding, and the experience. No rental cost, no build-out required.

The Distribution

Tomas becomes a sales channel from day one -- selling VITARE retreat packs directly to hotel guests and through his existing network. We reach a warm, pre-qualified audience of premium travellers without spending a euro on acquisition.

The Timing

June and July are peak season in the Costa Brava and Girona region. International visitors, high disposable income, wellness-forward mindset, and the perfect environment to road-test IV drip demand and supplement sales in a real hospitality setting.

The Offering

The Hypothesis

Three Questions The Pilot Answers

01

Is IV demand real?

We believe it is. But we want data, not assumption. Two months of selling IV drips to premium hotel guests in peak season will tell us exactly what converts, what doesn't, and what the average ticket looks like in real conditions.

02

Do supplements sell through hotels?

The retail supplement market is enormous but untested in this exact format. We find out if premium travellers buy curated protocol stacks when recommended by a trusted host -- and whether they reorder after their stay.

03

Does the VITARE name land?

Every guest who experiences the Girona pilot hears the VITARE name before Barcelona opens. We are seeding the brand in the exact audience that will become members -- months before the club exists. The pilot is also a pre-launch marketing campaign.

The Partner

Tomas + Tragaluz

This is not a cold partnership pitch. Tomas is a trusted relationship -- which means the pilot starts from a position of mutual trust, shared vision, and genuine enthusiasm for the concept.

Why Tragaluz

Tragaluz is one of the most respected hospitality groups in Spain -- known for design-forward, quality-first venues that attract exactly the type of guest VITARE is built for. Tomas's new hotel in Girona sits squarely in the premium segment. His guests are already predisposed to quality, experience, and health-conscious living.

What Tomas Brings

Beyond the physical space, Tomas brings his personal network, his credibility as an introducer, and his team's ability to recommend and sell the retreat packs to guests. In the right hands, a warm hotel recommendation converts at a rate no digital ad can match. Tomas is that warm hand.

The bigger picture: The Girona pilot is not just a revenue test -- it is the first chapter of the VITARE story. A summer in Girona with Tragaluz means the brand enters the Barcelona investment conversation already known, already respected, and already proven. That changes the risk profile of the full investment entirely.
The Logic

Move First. Prove It. Then Build.

The best investment pitch is not a business plan. It is a business plan backed by real results. The Girona pilot gives us both -- and it starts in May.
