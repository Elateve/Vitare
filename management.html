Legal Framework

Management Services Agreement

The term sheet below outlines the proposed relationship between the investor (Owner) and Elateve Strategy Group (Manager). This is the operating framework that governs how the centre is built, run, and monetised.

Clause 1 -- Appointment & Term

Engagement & Duration

Engagement

The Owner hereby engages the Manager to exclusively develop, launch, and operate the Biohacking Studio Longevity Centre located in the Jardinets de Gracia area, Barcelona.

Initial Term

Five (5) years from the Grand Opening Date. This gives both parties a meaningful runway to build the business, reach maturity, and demonstrate scalable returns.

Renewal

Automatic five (5) year extension unless terminated by either party with six (6) months' written notice prior to the end of the current term.

Clause 2 -- Roles & Obligations

Who Does What

The Owner (Investor)

  • Secures and holds the physical commercial lease for the premises
  • Provides 100% of the CAPEX (fit-out, equipment, deposits)
  • Provides the initial working capital buffer
  • Retains ownership of all physical assets (furniture, biohacking machinery, fixtures)

The Manager (Elateve)

  • Concept Owner: Holds the IP for the concept and franchise rights
  • Project Management: Full oversight of the "warm minimalist" design, construction, and equipment sourcing
  • Operations: Daily management of the facility, including hiring and training of medical staff (IV/testing)
  • Strategy & Growth: Management of the brand ecosystem, membership sales, and marketing via the Elateve app

Clause 4 -- Intellectual Property (IP)

Who Owns What

Manager Ownership

All intellectual property -- including the Biohacking Studio name, "4 Pillars" methodology, customer databases, proprietary software/app code, and operational manuals -- remains the 100% exclusive property of the Manager (Elateve).

Licensing

The Owner is granted a revocable, non-transferable license to use the IP strictly for the operation of this specific Barcelona location. The license is active for the duration of the management agreement.

Franchise Rights

The Manager reserves the absolute right to franchise or license the Biohacking Studio concept to third parties globally. This agreement does not constitute a partnership or equity stake in the parent Elateve brand.

Clause 5 -- Termination & Performance

Exit Provisions

For Cause

Either party may terminate for material breach, insolvency, or loss of required medical or operating licenses. Standard legal protections apply to both sides.

Performance Clause

The Owner may terminate the agreement if the Manager fails to achieve 80% of the agreed-upon EBITDA targets for two consecutive years, following a 12-month ramp-up period from Grand Opening.

Asset Handover

Upon termination, the Owner retains the lease and all physical equipment. However, all Biohacking Studio branding, signage, and proprietary software must be removed or returned within 30 days.

The Conclusion

A Turnkey High-Yield Asset

The investor provides the body -- the building and equipment. Elateve provides the soul -- the brand, the customers, and the expertise. The result: a professionally managed longevity centre that participates in one of the fastest-growing markets in the world, with zero operational burden on the investor.

This term sheet is provided for discussion purposes only and does not constitute a binding agreement.
