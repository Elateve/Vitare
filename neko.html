The VITARE Thesis

Why VITARE Goes Further

Neko answers the question "What is wrong?" VITARE answers that question and then provides the answer to "Now what do I do about it?" — all under one roof.

Diagnose and Treat

Neko's 15-minute scan tells you what is wrong, then sends you home with a referral. VITARE's diagnostic programme flows directly into personalised IV protocols, recovery sessions, and supplement prescriptions — all delivered in-house. The patient journey never leaves the building.

Monthly Revenue, Not Annual

Neko earns ~€299 per customer per year. VITARE earns €350–€850 per member per month, plus bar revenue, services, and corporate packages. A single VITARE member generates 14–34x more lifetime revenue than a single Neko scan customer.

Community Drives Retention

Neko's 80% annual rebooking rate is impressive. But VITARE's social model — the lounge, the bar, the events, the cold plunge ritual — creates daily and weekly touchpoints that make cancellation emotionally difficult. Members come for the science, stay for the people.

Franchise-Ready from Day 1

Neko required $325M in venture funding to build proprietary hardware. VITARE launches for €527k with commercially available equipment and a repeatable playbook. The model is designed to franchise across Mediterranean cities once Barcelona proves it.
