Part Six -- Agreement Terms

IP, Term & Termination

Appointment & Term

Owner engages Elateve exclusively to develop, launch, and operate the Biohacking Studio Longevity Centre in Jardinets de Gracia. Initial term: 5 years from Grand Opening. Automatic 5-year renewal unless terminated with 6 months' written notice.

Owner Obligations

Secures and holds the commercial lease. Provides 100% of CAPEX (fit-out, equipment, deposits) and initial working capital. Retains ownership of all physical assets (furniture, biohacking machinery, fixtures).

Elateve Obligations

Full project management (design, construction, procurement). Daily operations, hiring, medical compliance. Strategy and growth including membership sales, marketing via the Elateve app, and brand ecosystem management.

Manager IP

All IP -- including the Biohacking Studio name, "4 Pillars" methodology, customer databases, proprietary software, and operational manuals -- remains 100% exclusive property of Elateve.

Licensing & Franchise

Owner is granted a revocable, non-transferable license to use the IP strictly for this specific Barcelona location. Elateve reserves the absolute right to franchise or license the concept globally.

Termination & Performance

Either party may terminate for material breach, insolvency, or loss of medical licenses. Owner may terminate if Elateve fails to achieve 80% of EBITDA targets for two consecutive years after a 12-month ramp. On termination, Owner retains the lease and equipment; Biohacking Studio branding removed within 30 days.

Turnkey High-Yield Asset

The investor provides the body -- building and equipment. Elateve provides the soul -- brand, customers, and expertise. The result: a professionally managed longevity centre participating in one of the fastest-growing markets in the world, with zero operational burden on the investor.
